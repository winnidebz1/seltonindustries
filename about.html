About Selton Industries Limited

Building a legacy of quality manufacturing in Ghana

Who We Are

Selton Industries Limited is a food and beverages manufacturing company based in Spintex Road, Greater Accra. We focus on producing high-quality food and beverage products through structured processes, modern production methods, and strict quality control.

Our operations are built on the foundation of consistency, reliability, and scalability. We understand that successful partnerships require more than just products—they demand dependable supply chains, standardized processes, and unwavering commitment to quality.

From our facility in Accra, we serve distribution partners and B2B clients who value manufacturing excellence and operational integrity.

Our Approach

01

Process-Driven

Every aspect of our manufacturing operation follows documented procedures and standardized methodologies. This ensures consistency across production runs and enables scalability.

02

Capacity-Focused

We invest in infrastructure and systems that support growing demand. Our facility is designed to scale production while maintaining quality standards.

03

Quality-Controlled

Rigorous quality checks at every stage—from raw material sourcing to finished product packaging—ensure that our output meets consistent standards.

04

Partnership-Oriented

We view our clients as long-term partners. Our operations are structured to support reliable supply relationships and collaborative growth.
